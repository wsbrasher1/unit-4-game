aka Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four jewels below. By clicking on a jewel you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number shown. You lose the game of your total score goes above that of the target number.

The value of each jewel is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
